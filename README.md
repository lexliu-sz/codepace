# Codepace

Tap once for each character you type on your physical keyboard. Codepace measures your tap rhythm against real code snippets in Python, JavaScript, and Swift to display your live characters per minute. No sign-in, no cloud — just a snippet, your keyboard, and your current CPM.

iPhone & iPad. Free. No ads, no in-app purchases, no accounts, no network.
